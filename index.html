Privacy Policy – Shoqëria e Fjalëve

Last updated: January 2026

Shoqëria e Fjalëve (“we”, “our”, or “the app”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use our mobile game.

Information Collection and Use

While the game can be played as a guest without creating an account, we allow users to create an account to save their progress. If you choose to create an account, we collect the following personal information:

  • Email Address
  • Username

This information is used solely for authentication, account management, and social features (chat).

Account Deletion (Data Deletion)

If you have created an account, you have the full right to delete your account and all associated data.

  • 1. In-App Deletion: You can delete your account immediately by going to the Settings menu within the app and clicking the "Delete Account" button. This action is irreversible.
  • 2. Web Request: If you cannot access the app, you may request account deletion by contacting us via email at fjaleshloje@gmail.com.

Children’s Privacy

Shoqëria e Fjalëve is suitable for all ages. However, we do not knowingly collect personal data from children under the age of 13. If you believe that a child has provided personal information, please contact us and we will take appropriate action immediately.

Chat and Social Features

The app includes in-game social features such as chat and player interaction.

  • Messages are used only for gameplay and communication purposes.
  • Messages are not shared with third parties.
  • Chat messages are not used for advertising or profiling.

Third-Party Services

The app may use third-party services such as Google Play Services. These services may collect limited technical data in accordance with their own privacy policies.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect users and ensure a safe gaming environment.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
